About Us

Decades in retail.
Fresh eyes on design.

Arenaria Design was born from a simple idea: that good taste built over years in retail can translate into something people actually want to wear and live with.


Our team brings long experience from the physical retail world — understanding what people want, what catches the eye on a shelf, and what lasts beyond a trend. We're newcomers to e-commerce, and we embrace that. It keeps us curious, deliberate, and close to what our customers actually need.


Every product we release starts as a genuine design decision — a colour, a shape, a feeling — then gets printed fresh to order and shipped directly to you. No warehouses. No excess. Just thoughtful design on quality products.

S
Suleyman Emrah Cetinkaya
Founder & Designer — Arenaria Design / ARENARIA LLC
